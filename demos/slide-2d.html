normal state

state change

$('.slider').addClass('active');

Smooth as butter

By using 2d transformations instead of 'left' and 'top' properties, you allow the browser to manage the layer's animation as a separate composited unit, speeding things up.

This technique demonstrates why it's important to test on real devices - the iOS emulator runs this perfectly, but an iPad will blink white when repainting after the animation.
